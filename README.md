### Overview :

Rentyne is a car rental app built with Flutter and Dart, designed for people who love modified and premium cars.
It lets users easily browse, explore, and rent cars based on their favorite brands, all in one smooth and stylish app.
With Rentyne, users can check car details, view images and video previews, and rent their dream ride by choosing start and end dates.

### Key Features :

1. **Browse Modified Cars** : Explore a collection of premium and modified cars from different brands.
2. **Brand Category** : Easily find cars by your preferred brand.
3. **Car Details & Previews** : Check detailed descriptions, images, and video previews before renting.
4. **Book Your Ride** : Select start and end dates to rent cars in just a few taps.

   
### User Interface :

1. **Home Screen** : Browse all available cars and filter by brand.
2. **Car Details Screen** : View car details, images, and video previews.
3. **Booking Screen** : Pick dates and confirm your rental.

   
### Technology Stack :

1. **Flutter** : Cross-platform mobile framework for Android and iOS.
2. **Dart** : For efficient and smooth app logic.
3. **Third-Party Packages** : Used for UI components and state management.
